About

Orwig Tax Services is the practice of Scott Orwig, a CPA with more than a decade of experience in tax. Beyond client work, Scott teaches tax professionals across the country — leading continuing-education courses on individual, partnership, and business taxation — which means the advice you get reflects both current law and real-world practice.

Based in Colorado and working with clients remotely nationwide, the practice serves individuals and small businesses that want more than a once-a-year filing: clear answers, proactive planning, and honest guidance when a question goes beyond taxes.
