About

Orwig Tax Services is the practice of Scott Orwig, a CPA with more than a decade of experience in tax. His experience spans individual taxation, partnership and pass-through taxation, estate, gift, and trust taxation, and planning for high-net-worth individuals and families. Beyond client work, Scott teaches continuing-education courses to tax professionals across the country — so the advice you get reflects both current law and real-world experience.

Based in Colorado and working with clients remotely nationwide, the practice serves individuals and businesses that want more than a once-a-year filing: clear answers, proactive planning, and honest guidance when a question goes beyond taxes.
